Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We’ve compiled answers to the most common questions from both Culinary Professionals and Hospitality Employers to help you understand how our service works and how to get started.

For Chefs & Culinary Professionals

Any professional cook, chef, baker, pastry artist, or culinary graduate currently residing in Nigeria can register. We welcome both experienced chefs and passionate beginners who have completed basic culinary training or apprenticeships.

Simply visit our “Submit CV” or “I Need a Job!” page and fill out the registration form. Once submitted, our team will review your details, verify your experience, and add your profile to our database. You’ll then start receiving job alerts and career updates based on your qualifications.

Registration is free, but to enjoy full job access, notifications, and priority placement, you’ll need to choose a subscription plan.Plans start as low as ₦2,000/month, with discounts for longer durations.

• Access to verified job listings nationwide • Weekly job notifications by email • Priority visibility to top employers • Career and interview support • Invitation to culinary workshops and events

Once registered and subscribed, you’ll automatically receive notifications via email or SMS when matching vacancies are available.

Chefs.com.ng/CJA does not guarantee employment. We serve as an intermediary connecting culinary professionals with verified employers.Hiring decisions are made solely by the employer after interviews and assessments.

Yes. You can renew or upgrade your plan at any time from your user dashboard or by contacting our support team.

Currently, our services are available only to residents of Nigeria. However, we are expanding our network to other African countries soon.

For Employers & Hospitality Businesses

Any hotel, restaurant, eatery, catering company, private household, or event service within Nigeria looking to hire culinary professionals can register.

Visit our “I Need a Chef, Find Relief Chef, Find Permanent Chef, Find Private Chef, or Chefs Hub!” page, fill out the form specifying your requirements, and our team will match you with suitable, pre-screened candidates.

We recruit for all culinary levels: • Executive Chefs • Sous Chefs • Cooks & Kitchen Assistants • Pastry & Bakery Specialists • Private & Relief Chefs

Our employer subscription plans start from ₦5,000/month, giving you access to our verified chef database, job posting services, and candidate shortlisting support.

All chefs in our database go through a background verification process, including: • ID verification • Qualification or experience review • Reference checks (when available)
