Chef James & Associates (CJA) Recruitment Agency

Connecting Culinary Talent with Opportunities.

Welcome to Chefs.com.ng, the official recruitment arm of Chef James & Associates (CJA) — Nigeria’s premier niche recruitment agency dedicated exclusively to the culinary and hospitality industry. Based in the heart of Abuja, we are a team of veteran hospitality professionals, seasoned chefs, and culinary enthusiasts united by one passion: helping culinary talents find rewarding careers and helping employers discover the perfect fit for their kitchens and dining experiences.

At CJA, we believe that great food begins with great people. We work hand-in-hand with top hotel brands, restaurants, eateries, catering services, culinary schools, and event management firms across Nigeria and beyond. Whether you’re a skilled chef seeking a new challenge, or an employer searching for trained, reliable kitchen and service staff, our expertise ensures a seamless match that benefits both sides.

Our approach goes beyond recruitment — it’s about building culinary careers and nurturing hospitality excellence. We understand the pulse of the industry and are committed to raising its standards by connecting passion with purpose.

What We Do

  • Chef & Kitchen Staff Recruitment – From commis chefs to executive chefs, we connect you with the right team.
  • Hospitality Workforce Placement – We supply hotels, restaurants, and events with reliable, well-trained service staff.
  • Culinary Career Development – Partnering with training institutes to upskill aspiring chefs and hospitality professionals.
  • Consulting for Food Businesses – Helping new and existing culinary ventures build strong teams for sustainable growth.
Our Mission

To bridge the gap between talent and opportunity in Africa’s growing food and hospitality industry — ensuring every chef, kitchen professional, and food business thrives through the right connections.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted culinary and hospitality recruitment brand, known for connecting passionate talent with exceptional opportunities — inspiring a new generation of chefs and hospitality professionals who redefine excellence in every kitchen and service space.
