Privacy Policy

Get to know our Privacy and Policy

Effective Date: 18.10.2025   |   Last Updated: 18.10.2025

Chefs.com.ng / Chef James & Associates Nigeria Limited (CJA) Recruitment Agency (“CJA,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your data when you use our website or any of our recruitment, consultancy, or training services. By accessing or using our website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. WHO WE ARE

Chefs.com.ng / Chef James & Associates Nigeria Limited (CJA) Recruitment Agency is a hospitality-focused human resources and recruitment firm that connects qualified professionals with reputable employers across Nigeria and internationally. We operate under ethical recruitment standards and respect your right to privacy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name, gender, and contact details (phone number, email, address)
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV), employment history, and educational background
  • Professional certifications and references
  • National Identification Number (NIN), passport details (for international placements)
  • Employer or business details (for client accounts)
  • Payment information for service transactions

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Cookies and analytics data to enhance user experience

C. Information from Third Parties

  • Public job boards or professional networking platforms (e.g., LinkedIn)
  • Partner recruitment agencies
  • Referees or verification bodies

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Recruitment & Job Matching: To assess your suitability for job opportunities and share your profile with potential employers.
  • Client Service Delivery: To fulfill recruitment, outsourcing, or consultancy contracts with clients.
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, provide updates, or send service-related notifications.
  • Legal Compliance: To verify identity, prevent fraud, and comply with labor and immigration laws.
  • Improvement of Services: To analyze website usage trends and improve recruitment processes.
  • Marketing Communications (optional): With your consent, we may send newsletters or offers. You can unsubscribe anytime.

4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING DATA

  • Your consent through registration or submission of details.
  • Contractual necessity for recruitment or service delivery.
  • Legitimate interest for business and fraud prevention.
  • Legal obligation when required by authorities.

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

  • Employers or Clients seeking candidates for open positions.
  • Background check and verification agencies.
  • Service providers (IT support, hosting, payment processors).
  • Regulatory or law enforcement agencies, if required by law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information for marketing purposes.

6. DATA RETENTION

  • Data retained as long as necessary for recruitment or contractual obligations.
  • For up to five (5) years after your last interaction unless law requires longer.
  • You may request deletion via Section 11.

7. DATA SECURITY

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL)
  • Secure servers and access control
  • Regular system audits and restricted data access
  • Staff confidentiality agreements and data protection training

However, no system is completely secure, and online data transmission carries inherent risks.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access your personal data held by CJA
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Object to data processing for marketing
  • Request data portability

9. COOKIES AND ANALYTICS POLICY

We use cookies to enhance user experience, remember preferences, analyze traffic, and collect analytics data. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some site features may not work properly.

  • Monitor page performance and traffic.
  • Improve navigation and service quality.
  • Customize user experience.

10. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our site may contain links to third-party platforms. CJA is not responsible for their content or privacy practices. We advise reviewing their policies before sharing personal data.

14. CONSENT
