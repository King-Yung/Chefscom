CJA Verified Handle Program

"Stand Out. Get Noticed. Be Trusted."

The CJA Verified Handle authenticates and highlights genuine chefs, culinary professionals, and employers on Chef.com.ng. It serves as a badge of trust — confirming that your identity, experience, and professional credibility have been verified by Chef James & Associates (CJA).

Purpose

To build trust and transparency between culinary professionals and employers by confirming the authenticity of all registered members and boosting visibility for verified users across the platform.

How the Verified Handle Works

  • Register: Complete your chef or employer registration form.
  • Subscribe: Choose a verification package that suits your need.
  • Verify: Submit required documentation for identity and business confirmation.
  • Get Verified: Once approved, a blue CJA Verified Badge (✔️) appears beside your name or brand.
